How to make the perfect grilled cheese by Kris

Order #1

After you have your bread and cheese organised, heat up the pan to medium heat if you have not already. After the pan has heated up for a while, get a generous scrape of butter, place it on the spatula and spread the butter evenly all around the flat surface of the pan. Now you can either put the grilled cheese together before putting it in the pan (Buttered surface facing outside the sandwiches with your cheeses in the middle) and place it in the pan using a spatula or you can place it into the pan piece by piece using the spatula for the bread and your hands to place ye cheeses. The first method is recommended for less experienced chefs as it gives ye less risk of burning yourself while the second method gives you greater control of making the grilled cheese as ye’re taking ye time to place everything in its perfect position!
