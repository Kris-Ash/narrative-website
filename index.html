Order #1

Ah glad you’re on time chef! Today we have quite an order ahead of us, ready to cook the PERFECT grilled cheese chef? Aye! Glad to hear it, chef! First you’re gonna need all ye equipment and ingredients!

  • Cheese Grater/Knife
  • Butter Knife
  • Frying pan
  • Spatula
  • A plate
  • 2 slices of Sliced Bread
  • 50-70 grams of Cheese
  • Butter (and optional mayo)
  • Sauces
    •

Make sure all of ye equipment is clean and suitable for your workspace and ye food is before it’s used by date to make sure it’s proper tasty!

Now remember to keep in mind from here on out sailor if you’re ever unsure with the steps don’t be afraid to ask a responsible adult to help!

Tip #1

For the cheese I personally recommend the following combinations for a stellar experience:

  • ½ fresh mozzarella and ½ Cheddar
  • Red Leicester
  • And if ye want the true culinary experience you can use a cheese blend by combining Montgomery Jack, Edam, Mature Cheddar and Mozzarella

Tip #2

For your sauce I wouldn't just pull out my favourite sauce. Over the years I come to realise that you should compliment ye dish by maybe adding a sweeter sauce to cheese that is more mild or something bbq-ey to a creamier cheese.
