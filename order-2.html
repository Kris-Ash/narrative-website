Order #1

Let's get cooking! First if you need to preheat the stove to medium heat do so now so ye are prepared for what is to come!

Then take two slices of bread and spread a generous helping of

butter and mayo

the sides of the sliced bread that will face outwards when you eat it! From there lay both slices of bread butter side down next to each other on the plate (Do this gently so you don’t lose the excess butter or mayo). Then either use a cheese knife or cheese grater (depending on what you prefer to use or just have on hand) cut/grate all the cheese to your

desired sizes

 onto one of the slices of bread.
